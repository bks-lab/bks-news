Hosting

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service provided by GitHub Inc., 88 Colin P Kelly Jr St, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA.

GitHub is the recipient of your personal data. This corresponds to our legitimate interest within the meaning of Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. f GDPR in not having to maintain a server on our own premises. The server location is USA.

For more information on how to object and remove data with GitHub, please visit: GitHub Privacy Statement

GitHub has implemented compliance measures for international data transfers. These apply to all worldwide activities where GitHub processes personal data of individuals in the EU. These measures are based on the EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

Collection of Access Data (Server Log Files)

The hosting provider collects the following data in so-called log files, which your browser automatically transmits:

  • IP address
  • Address of the previously visited website (referrer)
  • Date and time of the request
  • Time zone difference to Greenwich Mean Time (GMT)
  • Content of the request (specific page)
  • HTTP status code
  • Amount of data transferred
  • Browser and operating system

The collection of this data is technically necessary to display the website and ensure stability and security. Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest).

As website operators, we do not have direct access to this data, but only receive anonymized, statistical summaries.

Cookies and Tracking

This website does not use cookies to track or analyze your behavior.

The only locally stored data is the theme setting (light/dark mode), which is stored in your browser's localStorage. This setting is solely for your convenience and is not transmitted to us.

No analytics tools (such as Google Analytics, Matomo, etc.) are used.

External Links and Content

This website contains links to external websites as well as embedded images from third-party providers. When accessing external links or loading embedded content, data may be transferred to the respective providers.

We have no influence on the data processing by third-party providers. Please refer to the privacy policies of the linked websites for information on how they handle your data.

Embedded Images

Our articles include images from external sources. When loading these images, your IP address is transmitted to the respective server. This is technically necessary to display the images. Legal basis: Art. 6 para. 1 sentence 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in displaying news content).

The following external image sources may be embedded:

  • News portals (e.g., Fortune, TechCrunch, Euronews)
  • Cloud storage (e.g., Google Cloud Storage)
  • Company websites of reported companies

The respective providers may set their own cookies or analyze your usage. We have no influence on this. If you have concerns, we recommend using a browser with tracking protection enabled or disabling images.

Your Rights

You have the following rights with respect to personal data concerning you:

Right of Access (Art. 15 GDPR)

You have the right to obtain confirmation as to whether we process personal data concerning you.

Right to Rectification (Art. 16 GDPR)

You have the right to request the rectification of inaccurate personal data.

Right to Erasure (Art. 17 GDPR)

You have the right to request the erasure of your personal data if one of the legal grounds applies.

Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18 GDPR)

You have the right to request the restriction of processing if certain conditions are met.

Right to Data Portability (Art. 20 GDPR)

You have the right to receive the data concerning you in a structured, commonly used and machine-readable format.

Right to Object (Art. 21 GDPR)

You have the right to object at any time, on grounds relating to your particular situation, to the processing.

Right to Lodge a Complaint (Art. 77 GDPR)

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority about our processing of personal data. The competent supervisory authority for us is:
